## Trust Building (Long-Term)

### What Builds Trust Over Time

1. **Consistency** - Regular blog posts, updates
2. **Transparency** - Open source, real code
3. **Products** - Working software people can use
4. **Honesty** - No fake claims, admit mistakes
5. **Responsiveness** - Reply to emails, engage

### What Destroys Trust

1. Fake testimonials (never do this)
2. Inflated claims ("10x developer")
3. Fake stats ("Trusted by 1000+ clients")
4. Stock photos as "clients"
5. Exaggerated results
